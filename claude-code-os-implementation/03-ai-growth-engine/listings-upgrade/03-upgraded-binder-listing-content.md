## Image Direction Briefs

### Main Image Brief

**Concept**: Premium product hero shot with Lifetime Warranty badge

**Requirements**:
- Product photographed at slight angle showing depth and quality
- High-contrast, dramatic lighting (dark background gradient)
- Scratch-resistant material quality visible (subtle sheen/reflection)
- Bold "LIFETIME WARRANTY" badge in lower-right corner (gold/metallic on dark background)
- Two binders shown (for 2-pack SKU) with slight offset
- Clean, professional composition - no clutter
- Resolution: Minimum 2000x2000px

**Reference Style**: Zulay Kitchen warranty badge placement, premium electronics product photography

**Do NOT**:
- Make it look like generic Amazon stock photography
- Use flat, even lighting (needs drama and contrast)
- Clutter with too many badges or text overlays
- Show product in isolation without visual quality signals

### Secondary Image 1: Card Protection Close-Up

**Concept**: Extreme close-up showing card in page pocket
**Purpose**: Demonstrate scratch-resistant quality at page level
**Requirements**:
- Macro-style shot of card sitting in 9-pocket page
- Visible premium material quality
- Card shown in perfect condition (mint)
- Subtle reflection/sheen on page material
- Text overlay: "Scratch-Resistant Premium Pages"

### Secondary Image 2: Gaming Context / Lifestyle

**Concept**: Binder in tournament/gaming environment
**Purpose**: Connect product to pre-game ritual and gaming identity
**Requirements**:
- Binder open on table alongside cards, playmat, dice
- Gaming environment visible (table, other players blurred in background)
- Dramatic, warm lighting (Friday Night Magic atmosphere)
- Player's hands interacting with binder naturally
- Text overlay: "Your Spellbook. Your Arsenal."

### Secondary Image 3: Two-Pack Value Display

**Concept**: Both binders shown with value messaging
**Purpose**: Communicate two-pack deal and versatility
**Requirements**:
- Both binders displayed side by side, slightly open
- Different card types in each (MTG in one, Pokemon in other)
- Clean white/neutral background
- Text overlay: "Two Premium Binders. One Smart Decision."
- Price anchoring visual (optional): "2x Premium Protection"

### Secondary Image 4: Warranty / Trust

**Concept**: Lifetime warranty visual
**Purpose**: Reinforce trust and differentiation
**Requirements**:
- Shield or badge graphic with "LIFETIME WARRANTY"
- Premium metallic aesthetic (gold, silver on dark)
- Supporting text: "We Stand Behind Every Product We Make"
- Professional, premium feel (not clipart or cheap graphics)

### Secondary Image 5: Feature Breakdown

**Concept**: Infographic-style feature callouts
**Purpose**: Quick-scan feature education
**Requirements**:
- Product shown with callout lines pointing to features
- Features to highlight:
  1. Scratch-Resistant Pages
  2. Durable Cover Material

**Concept**: Brand narrative image
