## Value Proposition Canvas

### Customer Profile

#### Jobs to Be Done
*What is the fantasy gamer/collector trying to accomplish?*

**Functional Jobs**:
1. Protect cards from scratches, bends, and damage
2. Organize collection for easy viewing and access
3. Transport deck safely to events and gatherings
4. Store cards securely long-term

**Social Jobs** (how they want to be perceived):
1. Be recognized as a serious, dedicated player
2. Earn respect from fellow players and opponents
3. Signal mastery and commitment to their community
4. Belong to the tribe of passionate gamers

**Emotional Jobs** (how they want to feel):
1. **Mastery & Recognition**: Pride in their skill and dedication
2. **Empowerment & Readiness**: Confidence before a match begins
3. **Story & Connection**: Deep attachment to the characters and moments in their collection
4. **Nostalgia & Escape**: Wonder and excitement stepping into the fantasy world
5. **Community & Belonging**: Part of something bigger than themselves

#### Pains
*What frustrates fantasy gamers about current storage solutions?*

**Severe Pains**:
1. Market is a "sea of undifferentiated competitors" - nothing exciting
2. Products treated as commodities with no emotional connection
3. Cheap options damage cards; expensive options feel like overpaying
4. Storage feels like a chore, not part of the experience

**Moderate Pains**:
1. Nothing communicates the excitement of the game
2. Products don't reflect their identity as serious players
3. Generic appearance doesn't inspire confidence or pride
4. No warranty or quality guarantees

**Minor Pains**:
1. Limited design options connected to fantasy gaming
2. Hard to compare quality before buying
3. Multi-pack options limited

#### Gains
*What would make fantasy gamers happy?*

**Required Gains** (must have):
1. Cards stay protected and scratch-free
2. Durable construction that lasts

**Expected Gains** (should have):
1. Professional appearance that signals quality
2. Easy to use and transport
3. Reasonable price for value delivered

**Desired Gains** (nice to have):
1. Lifetime warranty (confidence in quality)
2. Value bundles (Two Pack)
3. Fantasy-themed designs

**Unexpected Gains** (wow factor):
1. Feels like essential battle gear, not just storage
2. Transforms pre-game preparation into empowering ritual
3. Garners respect from fellow players and opponents
4. Becomes part of the gaming experience itself
