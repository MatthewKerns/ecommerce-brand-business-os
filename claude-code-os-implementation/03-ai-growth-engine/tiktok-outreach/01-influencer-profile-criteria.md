# TikTok Influencer Profile Criteria

## Who We're Looking For

Infinity Vault needs TikTok creators who authentically connect with the trading card and gaming community. The right influencer doesn't just have followers - they have a community that trusts their recommendations on gaming gear and collectibles.

---

## Ideal Influencer Profile

### Tier 1: Primary Targets (Highest Priority)

**TCG Content Creators**
- Content: Pack openings, deck techs, collection showcases, tournament vlogs
- Games: Pokemon, Magic: The Gathering, Yu-Gi-Oh, One Piece TCG
- Follower Range: 5,000 - 100,000 (micro to mid-tier)
- Engagement Rate: 3%+ (comments and shares, not just likes)
- Why: Direct audience overlap with our buyers

**Card Collection / Unboxing Creators**
- Content: Box breaks, pull highlights, collection tours, card grading content
- Focus: Pokemon and sports cards primarily
- Follower Range: 10,000 - 250,000
- Engagement Rate: 3%+
- Why: Their audience actively buys card protection products

### Tier 2: Secondary Targets

**Gaming Lifestyle Creators**
- Content: Game store visits, tournament day vlogs, gaming hauls, hobby room tours
- Style: Aspirational, community-oriented
- Follower Range: 10,000 - 500,000
- Engagement Rate: 2.5%+
- Why: Broader gaming audience that includes TCG players

**Tabletop / Board Game Creators**
- Content: Game reviews, game night content, hobby organization
- Style: Educational, enthusiastic
- Follower Range: 5,000 - 100,000
- Engagement Rate: 3%+
- Why: Adjacent audience with strong crossover to TCG

### Tier 3: Stretch Targets

**Pokemon Nostalgia / Pop Culture Creators**
- Content: Pokemon content broadly (not just TCG), nostalgia content, "adulting with hobbies"
- Style: Relatable, fun, nostalgic
- Follower Range: 50,000 - 1,000,000
- Engagement Rate: 2%+
- Why: Huge audience, Pokemon keyword volume is 10x other TCG

## Selection Criteria Scorecard

Rate each potential influencer on these factors:

| Criterion | Weight | Score (1-5) | Weighted Score |
|-----------|--------|-------------|----------------|
| **Audience Alignment**: Does their audience match our customer avatars? | 30% | | |
| **Content Quality**: Is their content well-produced and authentic? | 20% | | |
| **Engagement Rate**: Do followers actually interact (comments, shares)? | 20% | | |
| **Brand Fit**: Does their personal brand align with "battle-ready" energy? | 15% | | |
| **Collaboration Potential**: Do they do product reviews/integrations? | 10% | | |
| **Posting Consistency**: Do they post regularly (3+ times/week)? | 5% | | |
| **TOTAL** | 100% | | /5.0 |

### Score Thresholds
- **4.0+**: Immediate outreach - high priority
- **3.0-3.9**: Standard outreach - good candidate
- **2.0-2.9**: Monitor - may improve or fit a future campaign
- **Below 2.0**: Skip - not aligned

---

## Red Flags - Do NOT Partner With

- Creators who primarily do "drama" or controversy content
- Accounts with obvious bought followers (high followers, low engagement)
- Creators who promote competitor products exclusively
- Content that is low-quality or misrepresents products
- Creators with a history of brand disputes or negative professionalism
- Anyone who uses derogatory language about the gaming community

## Compensation Framework

| Tier | Followers | Suggested Offer |
|------|-----------|----------------|
| Nano (1K-5K) | Product gifting only | Free 2-pack binder |
| Micro (5K-25K) | Product + small fee | Free 2-pack + $50-100 |
| Mid (25K-100K) | Product + fee | Free 2-pack + $100-300 |
| Macro (100K-500K) | Product + fee + affiliate | Free 2-pack + $300-500 + commission |
| Major (500K+) | Custom deal | Negotiate based on reach and content plan |

**Affiliate Commission**: 10-15% on sales driven through unique code or link

**Product Seeding**: Send free product with no posting obligation (build relationship first, ask later)
