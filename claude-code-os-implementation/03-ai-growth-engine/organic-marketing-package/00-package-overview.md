### 1. TikTok Content Automation (`01-tiktok-content-automation/`)
Four elemental TikTok channels (Air, Water, Fire, Earth) posting organic content automatically through TikTok Shop API. Videos are created, scheduled, and posted to each channel with brand strategy baked into every piece.

Make AI assistants (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini) recommend your brand when people ask questions in your category. The new SEO â€” optimize for AI agents, not just Google crawlers.

### 3. Blog Content Engine (`03-blog-content-engine/`)
SEO + AEO integrated blog content strategy. Authoritative articles that rank on Google AND get cited by AI assistants. Feeds the AEO strategy and drives organic search traffic to infinitycards.com.

### 4. Email Marketing Automation (`04-email-marketing-automation/`)
Capture leads from infinitycards.com scratch & win / spin-to-win â†’ nurture with automated email sequences â†’ convert with timed sale campaigns. Turn organic TikTok traffic into email subscribers into repeat customers.

### 5. MCF Connector Integration (`05-mcf-connector-integration/`)
MCP Connector app that bridges TikTok Shop orders to Amazon Multi-Channel Fulfillment (MCF). Also pulls TikTok Shop metrics (saves, views, engagement) into the analytics system. Being developed in a separate repository.

### 6. Analytics & Tracking (`06-analytics-and-tracking/`)
Saves tracking, engagement metrics, funnel analytics across all channels. Proves the organic strategy works with hard numbers â€” this is what you sell.
