## Review Questions

### During Execution
1. Are we on track for the primary metric?
2. What are leading indicators telling us?
3. Any early warning signs to address?

### At Completion
1. Did we hit minimum viable success?
2. What metrics exceeded/missed target?
3. What would we measure differently next time?
