# Q1 2026 Quarterly Scaling Goal

## Accountability Group Commitment
**Date Set**: January 5, 2026

---

## THE ONE THING

> **Establish Infinity Vault's brand messaging foundation so we can deploy TikTok influencer marketing to sell through our binder inventory.**

### Daily Commitment
**1 hour per day, every day, for 90 days**

---

## Why This Goal?

### The Problem
- ~4,000 binders in stock that need to sell through
- "Battle-Ready Equipment" positioning exists on paper but is **invisible in the market**
- Can't arm influencers without clear messaging they can use
- Current Amazon presence looks like every other commodity binder
- Differentiation isn't communicated = low CTR, low conversion

### The Unlock
Once brand messaging is locked in:
1. Influencers know exactly how to position the product
2. TikTok content has a clear angle (not "here's another binder")
3. Amazon images, A+ content, and listing can be upgraded
4. Website copy becomes compelling
5. Every marketing dollar works harder

### Why Now?
- Inventory is sitting = cash is tied up
- TikTok TCG/gaming community is highly engaged
- Influencer seeding is relatively cheap to test
- Q1 is recovery season after holiday - perfect time to build foundation

---

## 90-Day Breakdown

### Month 1: January - Brand Messaging Foundation
**Focus**: Lock in the core messaging that defines Infinity Vault

#### Week 1-2: Research & Discovery
- [ ] Audit top 10 TCG influencers on TikTok (what do they say about gear?)
- [ ] Document what customers say in reviews (language mining)
- [ ] Study competitor messaging (what's the "sea of sameness"?)
- [ ] Interview 3-5 actual customers about why they bought

#### Week 3-4: Messaging Framework
- [ ] Define the core brand promise (1 sentence)
- [ ] Write the "Battle-Ready" brand story (why this matters to gamers)
- [ ] Create the key differentiators list (Lifetime Warranty, Scratch-Resistant, etc.)
- [ ] Draft the influencer brief template (how to talk about the product)
- [ ] Write 5-10 TikTok hook angles influencers could use

**Month 1 Deliverable**:
- Completed Brand Messaging Document
- Influencer Brief Template ready

---

### Month 2: February - Asset Creation
**Focus**: Turn messaging into visual and content assets

#### Week 5-6: Visual Assets
- [ ] Create new main image concepts with "Battle-Ready" identity
- [ ] Design lifestyle images showing product in gaming context
- [ ] Build A+ Content layouts with new messaging
- [ ] Create influencer media kit (logos, images, key messages)

#### Week 7-8: Content Assets
- [ ] Write updated Amazon listing copy with new messaging
- [ ] Create website landing page copy
- [ ] Draft 10 TikTok video script outlines for influencers
- [ ] Build email sequence for influencer outreach
- [ ] Create unboxing/reveal script template

**Month 2 Deliverable**:
- Updated Amazon creative assets (ready to deploy)
- Influencer Media Kit complete
- TikTok content templates ready

---

### Month 3: March - Influencer Deployment
**Focus**: Get products into influencer hands with clear direction

#### Week 9-10: Influencer Identification & Outreach
- [ ] Build list of 50 micro-influencers (10K-100K followers in TCG space)
- [ ] Personalize outreach for top 20 prospects
- [ ] Send first wave of outreach (10 influencers)
- [ ] Negotiate terms (product-only or product + small fee)

#### Week 11-12: Seeding & Tracking
- [ ] Ship product to first 5-10 confirmed influencers
- [ ] Provide brief and content guidelines
- [ ] Track content as it goes live
- [ ] Measure impact on Amazon traffic/sales
- [ ] Document learnings for next wave

**Month 3 Deliverable**:
- 5-10 influencers with product in hand
- First TikTok content live
- Initial performance data

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| "Too busy" to do the hour | Block it on calendar like a meeting; morning or evening consistent slot |
| Perfectionism on messaging | Set deadline - "good enough" by end of Week 4; can iterate later |
| No designer for assets | Use DesignForce subscription OR Fiverr specialists |
| Influencers don't respond | Send more outreach; try 50 to get 5-10 |
| TikTok content flops | This is learning; iterate on angles; small investment |
