## Objective

Build a systematic R&D engine that consistently generates, validates, and prepares new products for launch. Target: 1 validated, launch-ready product by end of Q1 2026.

### Success Criteria
- [ ] Generate 100+ product ideas
- [ ] Screen to 5-10 viable concepts
- [ ] Validate 2-3 via market testing
- [ ] Secure supplier for 1 validated product
- [ ] Have 1 product launch-ready
